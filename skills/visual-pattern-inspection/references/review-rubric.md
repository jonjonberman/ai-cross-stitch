# Review Rubric

## Categories (0-5 each)

1. Subject recognizability at stitch size
2. Silhouette clarity
3. Feature preservation (eyes, markings, key outlines)
4. Background simplicity
5. Color separation and contrast
6. Likely stitchability (low confetti, coherent regions)

## Scoring guidance

- 5: excellent, no obvious risk.
- 3: acceptable with moderate risk.
- 1: poor, likely unusable without major edits.
- 0: fails category.

## Automatic concern flags

- Subject appears unidentifiable after downscaling.
- Major features merge into background.
- Excessive tiny isolated details.
- Visual clutter dominates subject.
